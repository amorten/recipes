Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
1 cups brown sugar
2 cups sugar cubes
3 cups artificial sweetener
5 cups high-fructose corn syrup
8 cups honey
13 cups maple syrup
21 cups powdered sugar
34 cups marshmallows
55 pounds of bacon

Instructions:
Combine ingredients in large vat. Heat vat until vapors vaporate visibly. Cool. Eat.
